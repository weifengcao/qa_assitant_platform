# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may remove or reject contributions that violate it.
